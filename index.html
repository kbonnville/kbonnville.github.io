About

I’m a marine biologist and geographer integrating X-band SAR with optical datasets to map surface-water extent, lake area, and hydrologic change. I care about methods that are reproducible, interpretable, and ready for decision-makers. My recent work focuses on incidence-angle corrections, geolocation offsets, and multi-sensor fusion.

Beyond research, I teach geography labs, design clean data visualizations, and love mentoring students who are new to coding and GIS.
